Web3 - the future of the internet & crypto

Unlike Web2, where centralized companies control everything, Web3 gives YOU ownership over your assets, identity, and financial transactions.

What is web3?

Web3 is a decentralized version of the internet, built on blockchain technology. It eliminates the need for middlemen (banks, corporations, and platforms like Google, Facebook, or PayPal) by allowing users to control their own data, money, and online interactions.

  • ✓ Web1 (1990s): Read-only internet – basic websites, no user interaction.
  • ✓ Web2 (2000s-Present): Read & Write – social media, online payments, but corporations control everything.
  • ✓ Web3 (Now & Future): Read, Write & Own – Users own their digital identity, money, and assets without relying on banks or tech giants.

Think of it this way: Web2 is renting space on someone else’s platform (Facebook, YouTube), while Web3 is like owning your own land in the digital world.

Why does web3 matter for crypto traders?

Web3 makes crypto trading more secure, decentralized, and accessible.

  • ✓ No bank account? No problem—trade directly from a Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet).
  • ✓ No need to trust a central exchange—use DeFi platforms to trade, stake, or lend assets.
  • ✓ No restrictions—Web3 allows borderless finance, meaning anyone, anywhere can participate.

Example: Instead of trading on Binance or Coinbase (which can block access), you can use Uniswap, dYdX, or PancakeSwap, which operate fully on decentralized networks with no central authority.
