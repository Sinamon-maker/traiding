Essential Crypto Trading Resources

Market Data &Price Tracking

  • CoinMarketCap – Real-time prices, market caps, trading volumes, and rankings.
  • CoinGecko > – Comprehensive market tracking with fundamental insights.
  • CryptoCompare – Real-time data, portfolio tracking, and analysis.

Charting & Technical Analysis

  • TradingView – Professional-grade charting with indicators and analysis tools.
  • CryptoQuant – On-chain data & market analytics.
  • Glassnode – On-chain metrics, market intelligence, and deep analytics for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and altcoins.

News & Market Sentiment

  • CoinDesk – The latest crypto news, research, and industry trends.
  • The Block – Institutional-grade crypto news and insights.
  • Decrypt – Easy-to-digest crypto news & educational content.
  • CryptoPanic – Aggregated news and social media sentiment analysis.

Trading & Derivatives Platforms

  • Binance – The world’s largest crypto exchange with advanced trading features.
  • Bybit – Derivatives trading platform for futures and options.
  • FTX – Crypto exchange with spot and derivatives trading.
  • Kraken – A secure exchange for spot and futures trading.

On-Chain & Whale Watching Tools

  • Whale Alert – Tracks large crypto transactions and whale movements.
  • Santiment – Social and on-chain analytics for smarter trading.
  • Messari – In-depth crypto research and analytics platform.

Blockchain Explorers

  • Etherscan – Ethereum blockchain explorer for tracking transactions.
  • Blockchain.com – Bitcoin & multi-chain transaction explorer.
  • BscScan – Binance Smart Chain explorer.

Risk Management & Portfolio Tracking

  • CoinStats – Track your portfolio across multiple exchanges & wallets.
  • Delta – Portfolio tracking with real-time market updates.
  • Zerion – Manage your DeFi assets in one place.
